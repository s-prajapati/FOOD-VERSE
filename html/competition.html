    • GLOBAL CHEFS CHALLENGE

    1.
    The second runner-up will receive a Worldchefs Bronze medallion, trophy and a 3 piece selection of cookwares from AMT.

    2.
    The first runner-up will receive a Worldchefs Silver medallion, trophy and a 3 piece selection of cookwares from AMT.

    3.
    The Best Use of Dilmah Tea in Gastronomy winner will receive a Trophy, a fully paid trip to Sri Lanka and a course at Dilmah School of Tea in Sri Lanka.

    4.
    The Grand Winner of Global Chefs Challenge will receive the Global Chefs Challenge trophy and Gold Medallion, a cash prize of 2 500 EURO, a 2 night trip for 2 people to Monte Vibiano Castle (including transfer from Rome airport and meals) and a 3 piece selection of cookwares from AMT.

    GLOBAL PASTRY CHEFS CHALLENGE

    1.
    The second runner-up will receive a Worldchefs Bronze medallion, trophy and a 3 piece selection of cookwares from AMT.

    2.
    The first runner-up will receive a Worldchefs Silver medallion, trophy and a 3 piece selection of cookwares from AMT.

    3.
    The Grand Winner and Global Chef will receive the Global Pastry Trophy and Gold Medallion, a cash prize of 2 500 EURO, a 2 night trip for 2 people to Monte Vibiano Castle (including transfer from Rome airport and meals) and a 3 piece selection of cookwares from AMT.

    GLOBAL YOUNG CHEFS CHALLENGE

    3.
    The Grand Winner will receive the Hans Bueschkens Challenge trophy and Gold Medal, a cash prize of 1000 EURO, a 2 night trip for 2 people to Monte Vibiano Castle (including transfer from Rome airport and meals) and a 3 piece selection of cookwares from AMT.
